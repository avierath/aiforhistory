GPT is an acronym for “Generative Pre-trained Transformer.” A GPT generates new [data] in the image of the information on which it was trained. It uses an [algorithm] that is optimized to connect long strings of data. In the case of ChatGPT, this means it is incredibly adept at relating the data of sentences and paragraphs.
