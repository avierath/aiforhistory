title: "Reinforcement Learning"
related_terms: reinforcementlearning
---

Reinforcement learning is widely considered the most effective method to train the behaviors of artificial intelligence Following the same idea as the concept in psychology, this practice rewards good behaviors and penalizes bad ones. 

This can be done by human trainers before the AI system is public or is done by user feedback after the model is published. It was pioneered by DeepMind (Google).
