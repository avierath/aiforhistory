# Concerns about AI

Simply put: New things are scary.

## General Concerns

These are broad concerns about AI usage that are not specific to the discipline and profession of history.

## A Historian's Concerns

These are concerns about AI usage that are specific to the work of a historian. This primarily focuses on issues affecting the professionalization of research and subsequent written productions.

## A Teacher's Concerns

These are concerns about AI usage in the classroom and in the act of teaching history.
